We decided to create powerful but simple to use apps that can help your company excel.

The Calendar app is a great way for everyone to track their shifts and other's too! With an inbuilt holiday planner everone can request holidays and the managers can accept or decline them giving control to only the people you want.

The holiday app is a great way for everyone to track their holidays and other's too! With an inbuilt holiday planner everone can request holidays and the managers can accept or decline them giving control to only the people you want.

Meetings enables you to set company meetings. It allows you to set date, times and even topics of discussions. It can show you past, present and future meetings so you can manage your time most efficiently.

Messages allows you to chat with others in your workplace. Enabling you to connect with peers and share moments as well as plan shift swaps or plan meetings.

The store is where you can upload products that you may provide to your employees such as stationary or uniforms and they may request for these items to use.

Updates are where you can send company wide announcements to you employees notifying them on sales drives and campaign updates making sure all staff members are on the same page.
